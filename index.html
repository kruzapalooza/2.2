Me, Myself, and I'm Lost - Freshman Year: How the Kook Found His Cool

What's more dangerous: asteroids and space aliens, or swirlies and high school punks? Join Matt, an up-and-coming graphic artist, as he navigates the bizarre universe of his freshman year while relating in his sketchbook just how much high school feels like a bizarre space prison in which there are no rules, no order, and no friends.
